Who we are

A European home for independent, evidence-based reporting.

DII-Global Europe (Vereniging zonder winstoogmerk / Association sans but lucratif) is the European continuation of the Donetsk Institute of Information — an organisation that has produced independent media, trained journalists and documented the war against Ukraine since 2009. Registered in Belgium in 2024, we now bring that experience to the heart of the European Union.

Our work sits at the intersection of journalism, open-source research and public policy. We believe that in an era of industrial-scale disinformation, accurate, well-sourced information is a form of security — for democracies, for alliances, and for the people who defend them.

What we do

Three lines of work.

We keep the toolkit small and the standards high: monitor what is being manipulated, answer it with verified facts, and bring the right audiences into the room.

🛰️

Monitor

We track foreign information manipulation and disinformation targeting Ukraine, the EU and the transatlantic relationship, using open-source methods.

📑

Research & Rebut

We publish sourced research briefs and factual rebuttals — turning raw signals into material policymakers and journalists can actually use.

🤝

Convene

We run Brussels briefings and closed-door sessions that connect policymakers, journalists and business leaders around shared, verified facts.

Flagship programme

The Transatlantic Truth Lab.

Our flagship initiative is an independent, journalist-led analytical center identifying information manipulation aimed at the U.S.–EU relationship — and countering it with verified, data-driven stories of transatlantic innovation, energy security and shared prosperity.

  • 540+ documented foreign information-manipulation incidents tracked in 2025
  • Flagship research briefs on information integrity, critical minerals and energy security
  • A four-step method: monitor → verify → publish → convene
  • Briefings that bring policymakers, journalists and business leaders to one table
